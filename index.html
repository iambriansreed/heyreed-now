About

Heather and Brian met on Bumble in November 2022. Heather's patience paid off when Brian replied after three weeks, leading to a connection that sparked from their first coffee date. After their second date, they decided to exclusively date each other.

Heather jokingly proposed in March 2023, and Brian followed with a ring a month later. Both previously married, their focus is on unifying their families. They are excited to celebrate their wedding day with you!
